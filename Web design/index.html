About Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport played by two teams on a rectangular court divided by a net. The objective is to send the ball over the net and ground it on the opponent's side of the court while preventing the same effort by the opponent..

Basics :

  • Two teams compete to score points by grounding the ball on the opponent's side.
  • Commonly played indoors and on beaches.
  • Players must not let the ball touch the ground on their side.

More volley

  1. Volleyball is governed by organizations like FIVB
  2. Played on varios surfaces:
    • Indoor courts
    • Sand
    • Grass
  3. It requires skills such as serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking, and digging.
