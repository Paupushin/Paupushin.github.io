Meet Our Team

The Sunset Spikers is a competitive indoor volleyball team based in California. Founded in 2008, our team comprises passionate players who are dedicated to honing their skills and fostering camaraderie both on and off the court. We prioritize teamwork, sportsmanship, and continuous improvement, aiming to excel in local leagues and tournaments..

Achievements: Multiple championships in local leagues. Finalists in the California Regional Volleyball Tournament (2019). Recognized for outstanding sportsmanship and fair play awards. Values: Excellence: Striving for continuous improvement in skills and performance. Teamwork: Collaborating and supporting each other to achieve common goals. Respect: Treating teammates, opponents, and officials with dignity and fairness. Fun: Enjoying the game and creating memorable experiences together.
